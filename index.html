Why PrivaJet

Finance should be
private by default

PrivaJet combines a privacy-focused ERC-20 token with a shielded pool smart contract and a self-custodial desktop wallet — giving you full control over your financial data.

🔒

Shielded Transactions

Move assets through the PrivaJet shielded pool to break the on-chain link between sender and receiver addresses.

🗝️

Self-Custody

Your private keys never leave your device. No accounts, no KYC, no third-party custody — you own your funds entirely.

📖

Open Source

Every line of code — smart contracts and wallet — is open source and auditable. Trust math, not promises.

⚡

Ethereum Native

Built directly on Ethereum mainnet. No bridges, no wrapped tokens, no additional trust assumptions.

🛡️

Upgradeable Contracts

Core contracts use OpenZeppelin's upgradeable proxy pattern, enabling security fixes without migrating assets.

🌐

Vesting & Governance

A dedicated Vesting Manager contract ensures transparent, on-chain token distribution for team and contributors.

How It Works

Private in three steps

Using the PrivaJet shielded pool is straightforward — no specialist knowledge required.

1

Download the Wallet

Install the PRIVA Wallet on Windows. Your keys are generated locally and never transmitted anywhere.

2

Deposit into the Pool

Send PRIVA tokens into the shielded pool smart contract. Your deposit generates a private note only you hold.

3

Withdraw Privately

Use your private note to withdraw to any address. The on-chain link between deposit and withdrawal is broken.

4

Stay in Control

No middlemen, no custodians. PrivaJet smart contracts are immutable logic — they can't freeze or seize your funds.
